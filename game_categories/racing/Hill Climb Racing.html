Hill Climb Racing

Hill Climb Racing is a 2D racing game which is created by the Finnish studio Fingersoft for  Android, iOS,  Microsoft Windows, and Windows Phone in 2012. It can be said as one of the most addictive and entertaining physics based driving game ever made! The rules are quite simple, control your character to drive on hilly terrains, collect coins and gas to continue till the end of the journey. After finishing a level, you can upgrade your vehicles by buying new equipments or unlock a brand new vehicle for the next level. One thing that you should not forget that the fuel must be loaded continuously because once it reaches the red zone, the time you are in the game will end soon.
